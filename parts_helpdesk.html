PARts Help Desk
Support Portal

Created a customer support portal to allow customers to access extensive knowldge base and generate support tickets. This platform also facilitated direct Seller to Suppleir data feedback and communication.

In a bid to improve our customer support experience, we first reviewed our existing support platform and identified areas of improvement. Here, we discussed with our service provider to determine if it was possible to achieve the outcome that we required.

This resulted in lengthy research and discussion with potential support service providers which could facilitate the platform we desired. Once we settled on a final solution, we tested the service extensively and begun building the platform to cater towards our customer base.

The new support platform allowed our customers to create and manage support tickets. The platform also allowed PARts customers to access the extensive knowledge base regarding solution articles and tutorials. An extension of the PARts Help Desk was the PARts Community which allowed PARts customers to provide feedback and feature suggestions which could be voted on.
