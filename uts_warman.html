UTS Warman
Challenge

The Annual Warman Challenge invites engineering students to take on their obstacle course to clear a variety of objectives. Using mechanical and mechatronic design, we built a robot that placed us first at UTS in 2022.


Intrduction

The objective was to design a prototype robot that can autonomously deliver a payload across a chasm and return using mechanical and mechatronics system.

Project Scope

- Design and build a robot that is capable of traversing the track an completing all objectives.
- My components included design of the vehicle and claw to drive to an pick up the payload.
